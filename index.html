How it Works

State-of-the-art motion-detcting cameras and complex algorithms are used to spot offenders of the natural order. Those offenders are then tagged using RFID technology, which tracks the suspect using their state issued licenses or metro card. After that the video footage is forwarded to our staff who critique the style of the suspect. If their movements are deemed erratic or unnatural, a fine is immediately sent to the offender's home address.

Who we are

NYC is a city of 20 million. And with those kind of numbers laws are needed to keep the people in line. Our company will provide safety, comfort, and jobs to the community. All while keeping big government out of your lives. Don't worry, New York City, we've got you covered.

FAQ

Will I have to get a new license?
Only if you live above 100th St. on the East side and above 120th St. on the West.
Will your surveillance cameras be watching me?
Our surveillance team has strict orders to only watch the videos of those engaged in unnatural acts. As long as you are not engaging in such inappropriate behavior you have nothing to fear.
